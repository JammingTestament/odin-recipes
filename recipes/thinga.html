Chicken tinga on top of a tostada is a quick and delicious meal that is sure to satisfy every stomach!

We quickly poached strips of boneless chicken in broth (heavy on Knorr Pollo) for tender, juicy meat seasoned with black pepper and Mexican oregano.
The sauce combines simmered tomatoes (I prefer Roma Tomatoes), onion, garlic, and loads of chipolte chiles, blended with cilantro for freshness and richness.
Frying corn tortillas or pre-bought to construct your dish, topped with cheese, crema, raw or grilled or pickled onions with extra sauce served on the side.

Ingredients
  • Chicken - 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, trimmed and cut lengthwise into thirds
  • 2 garlic cloves, peeled
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 3/4 teaspoon table salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon table salt
  • Sauce: 3 Chipotle chiles, 1 white onion, 1 pound tomatoes, 2 garlic cloves, cilantro, 1/2 teaspoon table salt, 4 tablespoons of Knorr Pollo (chicken bouillon)
